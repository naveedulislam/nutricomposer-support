### Barcode Scanner
- Scan packaged food products using your iPhone camera

- Log foods to **Breakfast**, **Lunch**, **Dinner**, or **Snack**

### Focus Nutrients
- Highlight key nutrients on your dashboard
- Customize which nutrients matter most to you
- Quick at-a-glance view of your priorities

### Custom Nutrient Targets
- Set personalized daily targets for each nutrient
- Auto-calculate targets based on your profile (optional)
- Adjust targets to match your health goals

### Favorites and Recent Foods
- Save frequently eaten foods to Favorites
- Quick access to recently logged items

### Custom Food Creation
- Create your own foods with custom nutrient data

- Use AI Assistant with voice: say "Create [food name] with [nutrients]"
- Copy any existing food and edit it as custom (Copy to Custom feature)
- Perfect for recipes, homemade meals, or regional foods
- Save and reuse custom foods
- Build a personalized food library

### Breakdown of Nutrient Feature
- Tap any nutrient in All Nutrients or Focus Nutrients view to see "Breakdown of [Nutrient]"
- Shows which foods contributed to that nutrient's intake
- Displays each food's contribution amount and percentage of daily target
- Foods used multiple times are consolidated into a single entry with combined totals
- Progress bar shows RDA and Upper Limit (for vitamins/minerals with defined ULs)
- Color coding: orange (below RDA), green (met RDA), red (exceeding upper limit)
- AMDR indicators for macros (protein, carbs, fat): shows acceptable calorie distribution range

## Siri Integration

Control NutriComposer hands-free with **9 Siri commands**:

1. **"Check nutrients in NutriComposer"** - View your daily nutrient summary
2. **"What am I missing in NutriComposer"** - See nutrient deficiencies
3. **"Check calories in NutriComposer"** - Quick calorie check
4. **"Check water in NutriComposer"** - See water intake
5. **"Log water in NutriComposer"** - Add water to your log
6. **"Add food entry in NutriComposer"** - Open food logging
7. **"Track food in NutriComposer"** - Start food tracking
8. **"Suggest food in NutriComposer"** - Get AI food suggestions
9. **"What should I eat NutriComposer"** - Get meal recommendations

*Add shortcuts in Settings to enable Siri commands*

## Food Databases

### Open Food Facts (Default)
- Community-driven database of packaged foods
- Includes barcode scanning
- Regularly updated with new products
- Global food products

### FNDDS (Food and Nutrient Database for Dietary Studies)
- 5,400+ survey foods
- USDA-sourced nutritional data
- Common foods and recipes
- No API key required

### Branded Foods Database
- 296,000+ packaged products
- Brand-name foods and products
- Comprehensive nutritional information
- No API key required

### Foundation Foods
- High-quality USDA foundational data
- Detailed nutrient profiles
- **Requires free USDA API key** from [fdc.nal.usda.gov](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov)
- Most accurate nutritional data
