**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Your Privacy is Protected

> **Important**: NutriComposer's AI Assistant uses Apple Foundation Framework LLM which runs **100% locally on your device**. Your conversations, food data, and health information are NEVER sent to any external servers. Everything stays on your iPhone.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information.** NutriComposer does not require account creation, does not collect user data, and does not transmit personal information to our servers.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All of your data is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

- Food diary entries (meals logged for breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
- Custom foods you create
- Favorite foods
- Nutrient targets and goals
- Water intake tracking
- Settings and preferences
- All data stored as JSON files on your device

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself.

## Apple HealthKit Integration

If you enable Apple Health integration, NutriComposer will:

- **READ** calories burned and step count from Apple Health (if you grant permission)
- This data stays on your device and syncs only through Apple's secure HealthKit framework
- We do not store, transmit, or share your HealthKit data with any third parties
- You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings > Health > NutriComposer

## Third-Party Services

NutriComposer uses the following third-party services for food database searches:

### USDA FoodData Central API
- Only food search queries are sent to retrieve nutritional information
- No personal data is transmitted
- Optional - requires you to obtain a free API key

### Open Food Facts API
- Food name searches and barcode numbers are sent to retrieve nutritional information
- No personal data is transmitted
- Used to retrieve nutritional information for packaged foods

### Apple HealthKit
- If enabled, uses Apple's secure framework to read health data
- All data stays on your device
- Governed by Apple's privacy policies

## AI Assistant Privacy

The AI Assistant feature uses **Apple Foundation Framework LLM**:

- Runs **100% on-device** - requires iOS 26+ with Apple Intelligence
- No data is sent to external AI servers
- No cloud processing
- Your conversations and requests stay completely private on your device
- No logging, no tracking, no data collection

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share any data with third parties:

- No analytics services
- No tracking or advertising networks
- No data brokers
- No third-party integrations that collect personal data

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

### Export Your Data
- Export your food diary as CSV anytime
- Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data
- Save the CSV file to your preferred location

### Delete Your Data
- Delete all app data at any time
- Go to Settings > Data Management > Clear All Data
- Removes all food diary entries, custom foods, and settings

### Manage Permissions
- Control Apple Health access in iOS Settings > Health > NutriComposer
- Revoke permissions at any time

## Children's Privacy

NutriComposer does not collect any personal information from any user, regardless of age. The app does not require account creation, has no user registration, and stores all data locally on your device only.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
