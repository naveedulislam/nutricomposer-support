# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Getting Started

### Q: Is NutriComposer free?
**A:** Yes, NutriComposer is **100% free**. There are no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs. The app was created to help people improve their health, and the developer wants to keep it accessible to everyone.

### Q: Do I need to create an account?
**A:** No. NutriComposer does not require any account creation or login. All your data stays locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and simplicity.

### Q: Which devices support NutriComposer?
**A:** NutriComposer is available for iPhone running iOS 16 or later. The AI Assistant feature requires iOS 26+ with Apple Intelligence enabled.

### Q: How do I get started?
**A:** Simply download the app from the App Store, open it, and start searching for foods or scanning barcodes. You can begin logging your meals immediately without any setup.

## Food Databases

### Q: Which database should I use?
**A:** It depends on what you're looking for:
- **Open Food Facts** (default): Best for packaged and branded foods with barcode scanning
- **FNDDS**: Great for general foods, recipes, and survey foods (5,400+ items)
- **Branded Foods**: Large database of 296K packaged products
- **Foundation Foods**: High-quality USDA data (requires free API key)

### Q: How do I get a USDA API key?
**A:** 
1. Visit [fdc.nal.usda.gov](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov)
2. Sign up for a free account
3. Navigate to your API key section
4. Generate a new API key
5. Copy the key and paste it in NutriComposer Settings > USDA API Key

The process is free and takes just a few minutes.

### Q: Why can't I find a specific food?
**A:** Try these solutions:
- Switch to a different database using the dropdown menu
- Try alternative search terms or the food's generic name
- Use the barcode scanner if it's a packaged product
- Create a custom food entry with the nutritional information from the package

### Q: Can I add foods that aren't in the database?
**A:** Yes! Use the Custom Foods feature:
- Tap the **+** button in the Custom tab
- Enter the food name and nutritional information
- Save it for future use
- You can also use AI Assistant to create custom foods by voice

## AI Assistant

### Q: Is my data sent to AI servers?
**A:** **NO.** The AI Assistant uses **Apple Foundation Framework LLM** which runs **100% locally on your device**. Your conversations, food data, and health information are NEVER sent to external servers. Everything stays completely private on your iPhone.

### Q: Why isn't the AI Assistant working?
**A:** The AI Assistant requires:
- **iOS 26 or later**
- **Apple Intelligence** enabled on your device
- A compatible iPhone that supports Apple Intelligence

Check your iOS version in Settings > General > About. Enable Apple Intelligence in Settings > Apple Intelligence.

### Q: What can I ask the AI Assistant?
**A:** You can:
- Ask about your nutrient intake: "How much protein have I had today?"
- Get food suggestions: "What should I eat for lunch?"
- Log foods by voice: "Add 100g banana to breakfast"
- Create custom foods: "Create salmon with 166 cal, 26g protein"
- Ask for advice: "What am I missing in my diet?"

### Q: Does the AI Assistant require internet?
**A:** No. The AI runs entirely on your device, so it works even without an internet connection (though database searches may require connectivity).

## Privacy and Data

### Q: What data do you collect?
**A:** **None.** We do not collect any personal data, analytics, or usage information. We don't track you, show ads, or sell data. Everything stays on your device.

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes! Go to **Settings > Data Management > Export Data** to save your food diary as a CSV file. You can open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application.

### Q: Can I delete all my data?
**A:** Yes. Go to **Settings > Data Management > Clear All Data**. This will permanently remove all food diary entries, custom foods, and settings from your device.

### Q: Where is my data stored?
**A:** All data is stored locally on your iPhone as JSON files. Nothing is stored on external servers or in the cloud.

### Q: Can I transfer my data to a new phone?
**A:** Yes, through two methods:
1. **Export and Import:** Export your data as CSV, then import it on your new device
2. **iCloud Backup:** If you use iCloud backup, your app data should transfer when you restore your new device

## Apple Health

### Q: What health data does the app access?
**A:** NutriComposer only **reads** two types of data from Apple Health (if you grant permission):
- Calories burned
- Step count

We do not write any data to Apple Health or access other health information.

### Q: Is Apple Health required?
**A:** No, it's completely optional. You can use NutriComposer without connecting to Apple Health.

### Q: How do I disconnect Apple Health?
**A:** You can:
- Toggle off "Enable Apple Health" in NutriComposer Settings
- Or revoke permissions in iOS Settings > Health > NutriComposer

## Siri and Voice Commands

### Q: How do I set up Siri shortcuts?
**A:** 
1. Open NutriComposer
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Scroll to the **Siri Shortcuts** section
4. Tap "Add to Siri" for commands you want to use
5. Say the command to Siri anytime

### Q: What Siri commands are available?
**A:** NutriComposer supports 9 Siri commands:
- "Check nutrients in NutriComposer"
- "What am I missing in NutriComposer"
- "Check calories in NutriComposer"

- "Log water in NutriComposer"
- "Add food entry in NutriComposer"
- "Track food in NutriComposer"
- "Suggest food in NutriComposer"
- "What should I eat NutriComposer"

## Features and Functionality

### Q: How many nutrients can I track?
**A:** NutriComposer tracks **40+ nutrients**, including:
- Macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat, fiber)
- All major vitamins (A, C, D, E, K, B-complex)
- Essential minerals (calcium, iron, zinc, magnesium, potassium, etc.)
- Lipids (saturated fat, cholesterol, omega-3, omega-6)
- Essential amino acids

### Q: Can I set custom nutrient goals?
**A:** Yes! Go to Settings > Nutrient Targets to set custom daily goals for any nutrient. You can also use the auto-calculate feature based on your profile.

### Q: What is Focus Nutrients?
**A:** Focus Nutrients lets you highlight the nutrients that matter most to you on the dashboard. This gives you a quick at-a-glance view of your key nutrient intake.

### Q: How does the barcode scanner work?
**A:** 
1. Tap the **camera icon** in the search interface
2. Point your camera at the product's barcode
3. The app will scan and search Open Food Facts database
4. Select the matching product to view nutritional info

### Q: Can I track water intake?
**A:** Yes! Tap the **water drops icon** on the Dashboard to log your water intake. You can set a daily water goal in Settings.
