# Compose Your Daily Nutrient Intake

NutriComposer is a comprehensive iOS nutrition tracking app designed to help you take control of your health through detailed nutrient monitoring and intelligent food logging.

## Why NutriComposer?

### **100% FREE**
Created to help people improve their health. The developer struggled for a while to find a good solution and doesn't want others to face similar issues. No ads, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.

### **Privacy-First AI**
The AI Assistant uses Apple Foundation Framework LLM which runs **100% locally on your device**. NO data is ever sent to external servers. Your conversations, food data, and health information stay completely private.

### **40+ Nutrients**
Track vitamins, minerals, amino acids, macros, and more with comprehensive nutrient information from multiple trusted databases.

### **Apple Integration**
Seamlessly sync with Apple Health and use Siri voice commands to log foods, check nutrients, and manage your intake hands-free.

### **Barcode Scanner**
Scan packaged foods instantly using your camera to quickly log nutritional information from thousands of products.

### **Multiple Databases**
Access USDA FoodData Central, Open Food Facts, and FNDDS to find accurate nutritional data for any food you eat.
